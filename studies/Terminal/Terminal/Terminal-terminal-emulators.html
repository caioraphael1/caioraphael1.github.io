~Oh-my-Zsh

  • oh-my-zsh .

  • oh-my-zsh .

  • Oh My Zsh is a framework that simplifies customization and usage of Zsh. It is basically a collection of plugins, themes, and ready-to-use configurations.

  • Oh My Zsh enhances the user experience with Zsh by adding functionalities like:

    • Themes: Several ready-made themes to customize terminal appearance.

    • Plugins: A wide range of plugins that add extra functionalities (example: git, docker, auto-suggestions, etc.).

    • Ease of configuration: Simplifies the process of configuring Zsh with a centralized, easy-to-edit config file.
