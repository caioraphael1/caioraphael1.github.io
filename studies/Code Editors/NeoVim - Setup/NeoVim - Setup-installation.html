Configuration

  • In PowerShell or CMD:

    • mkdir $HOME\AppData\Local\nvim

      • No need to replace anything.

  • Neovim's configurations are located under the following paths, depending on your OS:

    • Linux and macOS:

      • $XDG_CONFIG_HOME/nvim

      • ~/.config/nvim

    • Windows:

      • cmd:

        • %localappdata%\nvim\

      • powershell:

        • $env:LOCALAPPDATA\nvim\
