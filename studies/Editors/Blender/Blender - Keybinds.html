Names that appear in shortcut editing

  • Grless

    • . \

  • ;

    • Ã‡

    • `

    • Corresponds to quotes (' or ").

  • NDOF

    • Refers to a 3D Mouse.

  • Application

    • ~Unknown.

  • Tweak

    • "Mouse click and drag (optionally map drag direction to different actions)".

  • Timer

    • "Used to control actions based on a time period. E.g. by default, 'Animation Step' uses 'Timer 0', 'Smooth View' uses 'Timer 1'".

Shortcuts that reset when changed

  • All shortcuts related to the NumPad in the 'Node Editor'.

  • The Fill shortcut from 'Addon F2', always on the F key.

    • When exporting a new keymap, a copy of the shortcut is always created, accumulating over time.

Visibility

  • H

    • Hide selected object/collection.

  • alt H

    • Reveal all objects/collections.

  • Space

    • Navigate to the active element.

  • *Disclaimer:

    • All other 'hide/show' shortcuts are uninteresting. I couldn't make shortcuts that reproduce the Hide/Show functionalities of the '3D View/Object Mode'.

Expand and Collapse

  • 'Right Arrow'

    • Fully expand the selected object.

  • 'Left Arrow'

    • Fully collapse the selected object.

  • ctrl 'Right Arrow'

    • Expand all objects, one hierarchy at a time.

  • ctrl 'Left Arrow'

    • Collapse all objects, one hierarchy at a time.
