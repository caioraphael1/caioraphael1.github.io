Panics

  • assert .

    • Can be ignored with ODIN_DISABLE_ASSERT .

    • Closes the program.

  • ensure .

    • Cannot be ignored with ODIN_DISABLE_ASSERT .

    • Is stronger than assert .

    • Closes the program.

  • panic .

    • Closes the program.

  • unimplemented .

    • ?

  • log.fatal .

    • Does not close the program.
