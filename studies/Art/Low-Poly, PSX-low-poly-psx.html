  • Pixel Perfect grid and Changing the Grid to make it PixelPerfect {2:01 -> 5:00} / Use ready palettes while painting {21:03 -> 22:14} .

    • Addons: Texel Density and Sprytile Painter (for auto-update in Blender when saving the image in an external program).

    • For the Grid:

      • In 3D View / Overlay :

        • Scale: 0.0625  and Subdivisions: 16

        • Changing Scale does not seem to do anything. I don't know why it's done.

      • In Properties :

        • Scene -> Units -> Unit System: None .

      • Even in Blender 4.1.0, there is the problem of Scale not snapping to the grid.
