Recorder (Flute Doce)

  • Usually in C or F.

  • A type of woodwind instrument. It comes in different sizes (sopranino, soprano, alto, tenor, bass, etc.). The recorder family covers a wide pitch range.

  • Baroque vs German :

    • Baroque :

      • Has a B on the back.

      • F is played with a forked fingering.

    • German :

      • Came after the Baroque version.

      • Has a G on the back.

      • F no longer uses forked fingering.

      • Apparently the German system originated from a historical mistake in the 20th century.

    • "Although it is easier to play F on the German recorder, some experts say it is out of tune, so they recommend the Baroque system."

    • There is a difference in fingering due to the position of one of the holes.
