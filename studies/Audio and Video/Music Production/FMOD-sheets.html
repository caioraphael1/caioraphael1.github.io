Timeline Sheet

  • Play sound left to right.

  • If no loop regions are added, the timeline will play until the end of the last sound.

Parameter Sheet

  • Hitting play on the parameter sheet will not making the playback position move forward. The playback position is controlled by the current parameter value.

Action Sheet

  • Triggers sounds as soon as the event starts, either consecutively or concurrently.

  • It doesn't use a playback position.

  • Used for simple sounds that doesn't require the playback position.

  • To make a sound loop, click on it inside the Action Sheet and you'll see it's properties down below.
