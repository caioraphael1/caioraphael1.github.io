Idiophone

üïí Created: 2023-12-15 | Updated: 2026-02-01

  • Produces sound primarily through the vibration of its own material, without the use of strings, membranes, or external resonators.

  • Key characteristics:

    • The material itself vibrates to generate sound (e.g., wood, metal, or stone).

    • Can be struck, shaken, scraped, or plucked.

    • No tensioned membrane or string is needed.

  • Examples:

    • Xylophone (struck wooden bars)

    • Vibraphone (struck metal bars)

    • Marimba

    • Glockenspiel

    • Triangle

    • Cymbals
