Data Transfer and Error Detection

  • Excellent 5-video playlist by BenEater .

  • The following data transfer methods are suggested: 'NRZ (non-return-to-zero)' and 'RZ (return-to-zero)'.

  • The following error checking options are explained: 'Parity', 'Checksum', "CPF-style verification", 'Hamming Distance' and 'CRC'.

  • Video 4 talks about CRC, which makes everything much more complicated by involving multiple polynomial divisions and the "invention of a binary algebra". This video is not that interesting.

  • Video 5 clarifies some things from video 4, by using 'bit shifting' (with flip-flops) and 'xor' as operations to find the CRC.
